The Living Room

704 West High Street  ·  Urbana, Illinois
I
"We got permission to paint the walls as long as we painted them back. So we painted everything. We painted a lot of stuff green. And we had skateboards hanging around."
— Ken Negus, who lived at 704
II
"We would take all of the furniture out of the main areas, including the kitchen table, and we would push everything into the bedrooms on the first floor and padlock all the bedrooms."
— Kim Moore, who lived at 808 California and organized shows there
III
"It was unbelievable how amazing they sounded in a living room in an old house. I mean, it was just like crazy, absolutely crazy."
— Rachael Dietkus, who also lived at 808 California, on seeing Lungfish perform
IV
"We practiced at 107 and a half West Washington, which is now a hole in the ground.... Every American Football song from [LP1] was written in that house. I had a drum set up in the living room."
— Steve Lamos, American Football drummer
